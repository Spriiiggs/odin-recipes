Savage Eggs

Eggs For A Barbarian

These eggs will satisfy your immense hunger, help you build and repair worn out major muscle groups, and taste absolutely delicious! You will need:

  • 6-8 eggs
  • 3-4oz. diced steak
  • 2oz. crumbled cooked bacon
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped peppers
  • hot sauce of choice

Steps

  • Sautee peppers and onions in butter or olive oil
  • While cooking, whisk eggs together in a bowl
  • When peppers and onions are cooked to desire, add steak and bacon
  • Pour in eggs
  • Turn consistently for pile-o-eggs, or let sit until foldable
  • Serve on plate; douse in hot sauce
  • consume vigorously
