Liver King Lunch

The King's Superfood

It's not gross. This raw assortment of pasture-raised cow parts will act like rocket fuel to your health. You will need:

  • 1 Ribeye
  • 4-8oz. Raw beef liver
  • 2-3 Raw marrow bones

Add a pinch of salt as desired. Feel free to sear both faces of the ribeye (cooking well-done is strictly prohibited!). If the liver is too much to stomach on its own, dice into bite-sized chunks and drizzle with maple syrup, or take chunks down like pills with a drink. Marrow best eaten raw, but can also be baked at 425 degrees to soften, then spread on whole-grain toast.

Steps

  • Sear steak
  • Thaw liver (dice if needed)
  • Thaw marrow bones
  • Consume all (actual bones excluded)

The benefits of eating liver are abundant

  • High in bioavailable vitamin A
  • Great source of EPA and DHA
  • Contains all the B vitamins, very high in B12
  • source of bioavailable iron
  • contains trace minerals like copper

Bone marrow yeilds further life-enhancing benefits.

  • Contains adiponectin, reducing risk of diabetes and cardiovascular disease
  • Boosts immune system
  • Increases collagen and antioxidants
  • Incredibly nutrient-dense and delicious
